❓|About us:

As part of the Updating society of Tanzania Enterprises Group, we are a Youth led registered medical clinic that seeks to transform health and well-being of people through innovative evidence based practice. We are based in Dar es salaam, Tanzania.

As medical professionals, our core objective is to promote modern quality patient centered care. Through data science, informatics and research we seek to treat people with numbers and evidence. While, as public health specialists we also seek to treat people with community led impactful interventions. On the long run promoting health equity and self-advocacy.
