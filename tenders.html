Partnership Call: HealthTech Innovation in Tanzania

Join us in building impact-driven health technology solutions for Tanzania and Africa. We're seeking passionate technical partners to co-create the future of healthcare.

Are you passionate about AI, data science, or robotics and interested in applying technology to solve real-world healthcare and public health challenges? We are building an impact-driven health technology venture focused on Tanzania and Africa, and we are looking for a technical partner / co-founder to build with us.

Focus Areas

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) & Machine Learning for healthcare diagnostics
  • Data Science & Health Analytics for public health insights
  • Robotics & Automation for medical procedures and logistics
  • Digital Health Platforms & Clinical Decision Support Systems

Ideal Candidate Profile

  • Background in Computer Science, Data Science, Engineering, or related technical fields
  • Hands-on experience with AI/ML frameworks, robotics systems, or data analytics platforms
  • Motivated by impact-driven innovation in healthcare rather than just commercial success
  • Open to long-term collaboration, shared ownership, and equity participation
  • Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work in resource-constrained environments

Early-career innovators, recent graduates, and graduate students are strongly encouraged to apply.

Partnership Benefits

  • Co-creation of real-world healthcare solutions with measurable impact
  • Direct exposure to clinical challenges and public health needs in Tanzania
  • Mentorship from experienced healthcare professionals and technologists
  • Opportunity to grow into a founding technical leadership role
  • Access to network of healthcare providers, researchers, and potential funders
  • Equity stake in the venture for committed long-term partners
