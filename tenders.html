Partnership Call: HealthTech Innovation in Tanzania

Are you passionate about AI, data science, or robotics and interested in applying technology to solve real-world healthcare and public health challenges? We are building an impact-driven health technology venture focused on Tanzania and Africa, and we are looking for a technical partner / co-founder to build with us.

Focus Areas:

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) & Machine Learning
  • Data Science & Health Analytics
  • Robotics & Automation for Healthcare
  • Digital Health & Decision Support Systems

Who Should Apply:

  • Background in Computer Science, Data Science, Engineering, or related fields
  • Experience or strong interest in AI, machine learning, robotics, or data-driven systems
  • Motivated by impact-driven innovation in healthcare
  • Open to long-term collaboration and shared ownership

Early-career innovators and recent graduates are strongly encouraged to apply.

What This Partnership Offers:

  • Co-creation of real-world healthcare solutions
  • Exposure to real clinical and public health challenges
  • Mentorship from a Medical Doctor & Public Health Informatician
  • Opportunity to grow into a founding technical leadership role

How to Express Interest:

Send a brief introduction about yourself, your technical background, and your interest in healthcare innovation to:
partnerships@yourorganization.org
