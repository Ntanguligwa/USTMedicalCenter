Construction Tender - Building New Community Center

We are inviting eligible contractors to submit their bids for the construction of a modern community center in Arusha. The tender includes design, supply of materials, and construction work.

Scope of Work:

  • Building construction (approx. 1500 sq meters)
  • Landscaping and parking area setup
  • Fittings and furniture

Deadline:

Submissions must be received by 30th June 2025.

How to Apply:

Send your detailed proposal to tenders@hatuakwahatua.org or drop it physically at our main office during working hours.
